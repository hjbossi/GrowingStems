Welcome to the Growing STEMs Scholarship!

Our Mission

We believe that a diverse set of ideas, backgrounds, and experiences is critical in any initiative to advance knowledge. STEM fields show a well-documented lack of diversity, particularly for women and gender minority students. This underrepresentation is especially prominent for students who identify with multiple minority groups. Through this scholarship, we aim to support passionate and skilled students who not only demonstrate potential to excel in STEM fields, but also exhibit an aptitude for continuing our mission of bringing much needed diversity to their discipline.

Scholarship

We support students through a financial scholarship of $1,000 each. Eligible students are welcome to apply here. Applications who come from multiple underrepresented identities are especially encouraged to apply.

Eligibility

  • current high school senior
  • planning to pursue a course of study in a STEM discipline at an accredited post-secondary institution
  • identify as female or another gender minority in STEM fields
