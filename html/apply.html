Apply Now!

To apply please send your application materials in pdf form to growingstems4stem@gmail.com by April 30th, 2021. Applications will be reviewed by a committee and the recipient of the scholarship will be notified by May 31st at the latest.

Application Materials

  • resume
  • transcript (unofficial transcripts also accepted)
  • 1 reference (should be submitted separately by the reference)
  • personal statement (see prompt below)

Personal Statement Prompt

Please tell us in up to 500 words, why providing you with this scholarship would help us to further our mission (see here). Be sure to explain why you're passionate about STEM and why you bring a diversity of thought, experience, or background! Your grammar and resume need not be perfect, we're just looking for enthusiasm and potential for growth!
