Personal Statement Prompt

Please tell us in around 400 words, why providing you with this scholarship would allow us to fulfill our mission (see here). Be sure to explain why you're passionate about STEM and why you bring a diversity of thought, experience, or background! Your grammar and resume need not be perfect, we're just looking for enthusiasm and potential for growth!
